About FTC and RoboRavens

WHAT IS FIRST? FTC? For the Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology or FIRST® is an international organization that runs robotics programs for students of all ages. FIRST prepares young people for the future through a suite of life-changing youth robotics programs that build skills, confidence, and resilience. FTC or FIRST® Tech Challenge is a robotics competition which teams compete in an alliance format using robots build from a standardized set of parts. Teams design their robot to fulfill the tasks needed to be completed by the competition.
